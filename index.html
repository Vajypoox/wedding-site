Our Love Story

First Meeting

We met in college during a literature class. James spilled coffee on Emma's favorite book, and instead of getting angry, she laughed it off. That moment sparked a friendship that would blossom into love.

First Date

Our first date was at a small Italian restaurant downtown. We talked for hours, completely losing track of time. That night, we both knew there was something special between us.

The Proposal

On a trip to Paris, James proposed at sunrise in front of the Eiffel Tower. With tears in her eyes, Emma said yes, and we celebrated with croissants and champagne at a nearby café.
